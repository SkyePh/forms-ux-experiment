Form UX Experiment

You will try the same form twice: once all at once, and once using a stepper.

Before you start

This experiment compares two user experiences for the same ~15-field form: all fields at once vs a stepper (grouped categories).

After you finish, please complete the questionnaire and share which version felt easier and less tedious.

Title

Evaluating Step-Based vs Single-Page Forms: Impact on User Experience in Web Applications

Hypothesis

I hypothesize that completing the same form with a stepper will improve perceived usability (it will feel less tedious and clearer) compared to entering everything on one page.

Plan

Each participant will complete the form twice in the browser: first the all-at-once version, then the stepper version. After finishing, they will complete a questionnaire and we will analyze which format users preferred and found easier.
